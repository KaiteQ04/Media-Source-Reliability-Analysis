The NewsInfo dataset contains the following fields:

NewsID: A unique identifier assigned to each news article.
DeclareDate: The date on which the news was published or announced.
Title: The headline or title of the news article.
FullDeclareDate: The full timestamp of publication, including both date and time.
Author: The author or contributor of the article.
KeyWord: Keywords associated with the article.
NewsSummary: A brief summary of the news content.
NewsContent: The full text body of the article.
NewsSourceID: An identifier for the news source.
NewsSource: The name of the organization providing the news.
RecommendSign: A flag indicating whether the article is recommended (if applicable).
IsAccessory: Indicates whether the article includes additional attachments.
FileSize: The size of the text or file associated with the article.
