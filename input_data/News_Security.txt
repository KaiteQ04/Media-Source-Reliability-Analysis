The News_Security dataset contains the following fields:

NewsID: A unique identifier for each news article.
DeclareDate: The publication or announcement date of the news.
Title: The headline or title of the news article.
Symbol: The security code associated with the company or asset.
ShortName: The short name or abbreviated name of the security.
SecurityTypeID: The coded identifier representing the security category.
SecurityType: The category of the security, such as A-shares, B-shares, or Hong Kong stocks.
FullDeclareDate: The full timestamp of the news publication, including both date and time.
